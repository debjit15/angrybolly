About this game

angryBolly is a darkly humorous, fast-paced endless runner inspired by Bollywood energy and internet memes. Race through absurd obstacle courses, collect power-ups, and face explosive boss sequences.

Mechanics: magnet pickups, speed boosts, temporary shields, wormholes that teleport you forward (or backwards), blackhole traps, and stage-based mini-boss battles that change gameplay tempo.
